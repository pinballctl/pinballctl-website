Privacy Policy

This section applies to the Pinball CTL website. We do not collect or store personal data through this website.

Website Privacy

  • We do not ask for account registration or personal profile data on this website.
  • We do not intentionally collect or store personal data through contact forms on this website.
  • Basic server/network logs may exist at hosting level for security and operational purposes.
  • We may use analytics tooling (for example Google Analytics or similar) to understand aggregate website usage.

Analytics

  • Any analytics use is intended for high-level site performance and content improvement.
  • Analytics data is treated as aggregated usage information, not user profiling data.
  • You can restrict analytics tracking through browser privacy settings and blocker tools.

Pinball CTL Application Data (Local Runtime)

  • Pinball CTL is local-first: runtime configuration and machine data are stored on your own host.
  • Logs, rules, mapping, and media/runtime state are maintained locally by the operator installation.
  • Project import/export bundles are generated and controlled by the operator.
  • Remote services are optional and environment-dependent; operators control if/when they are used.

Operator Responsibility

  • Secure your host, local network, and backups according to your deployment requirements.
  • Protect admin credentials and exported bundles.
  • Review your own legal/compliance obligations for deployed installations.
